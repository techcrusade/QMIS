Our Mission

Our aim is to monitor the Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI) in rural areas, with a specific focus on industries. We provide real-time data and predictive AQI and WQI values on our webpage to empower government officials and stakeholders with actionable insights for preserving clean air and water in our communities.

User-Friendly Web Interface

Our webpage provides an easily accessible to monitor AQI & WQI in real time. This dynamic interface empowers officials to stay informed and take necessary actions promptly. It is also integrated with our database which contains all the observations read using sensors in real life.

AQI & WQI

Our advanced sensors constantly track and analyze the air quality parameters and water quality parameters from industries. This data is instantly converted into live data analyses for generating AQI and WQI displayed on our intuitive webpage.

    


About Us

Quality Index Monitor

Our objective is to assess and oversee the Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI) in rural regions, with a particular emphasis on industrial areas. Our system continuously records current data and forecasts AQI and WQI values on our website. This predictive feature enables us to offer advanced guidance to government authorities and concerned parties, equipping them with valuable information to make well-informed choices and safeguard the availability of clean air and water for our communities.
